Data Science Interview Prep App

Often, I find myself with free moments away from computers and books, yet eager to enhance my data science expertise. To bridge this gap, I developed a data science preparation app. With it, as long as I have cellular service, I can delve into various data science concepts, anytime and anywhere. I have used this in the Gym, on long car rides, and while waiting in lines just to name a few.

Technology Stack:

  • SwiftUI
  • OpenAI API

Selection Interface

The interface of all the different concepts you can learn or revisit to solidify your Data Science knowledge.

OpenAI API Interface

I successfully integrated ChatGPT into the app using OpenAI's ChatGPT API. This feature enables users to voice their answers, which ChatGPT then evaluates. It not only grades the response but also provides the ideal answer a data professional might offer during an interview. Additionally, it highlights areas of improvement in the user's answer.
